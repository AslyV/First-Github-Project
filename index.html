About me

Hello, I'm Asly

I am a proud girly of the natural hair community. Growing up as an Afro-latina from the Domican Republic,my hair has always played a big role in my life. At an early age my mom started perming my hair to fit western beauty standards. She wasn't educated on how to take care of my hair and often reffered to it as "pelo malo", which translates to bad hair. This made me view my hair as someting that needed to fixed or control. All my memories of getting my hair done as a child are full of pain, shame, and discomfort.
